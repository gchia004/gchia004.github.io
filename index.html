about us

Alternate Tone is a contemporary music school that is located in the central part of Singapore. We specialize in providing pop/jazz piano, electric and acoustic guitar. The school started out in 2009 and has schooled many people of all ages. As an extension, AT has introduces AT:Kids to offer specialised classes and directed learning to children between the ages of 1-10. We believe that every child is special and unique with their own muscial flair.

why choose us

We don’t believe in a one-size-fit all teaching plan. Every music lesson is customized to individual student’s needs and aspirations. At AT:Kids, we want to cultivate and foster genuine interest of music in children at a young age. We offer programes that will develop your child such as, cognitive, social-emotional and physical development, whislt growing their love for music

Music Lessons

At AT:Kids, our programs include "Groovy babies", "Stomp together", "Flow to the Rhythm" and "Young Musos" according to the age of your child. We focus on getting our kids to feel the music for themselves and to experiment different methods or learning and exposure of instruments.
